What is Parkour?

Parkour is the act of moving from point “a” to point “b” using the obstacles in your path to increase your efficiency. It has since then developed to be an expression of freedom of movement, where athletes incorporate more self-expression, improvisation, and the use of movements for aesthetics-only, such as flips and twists.

Movements

The most commonly used Parkour movements can be broken into two basic categories:

Vaults

Vaults are different ways of getting over an object in your path. There are many different vaults. These are a few.

  • Safety Vault
  • Reverse Safety Vault
  • Speed Vault
  • Kong
  • Lazy Vault

Rolls

Rolls are some of the most important tools in a freerunner's arsenal as it is allows them to safely land and contrinue their momentum after large jumps
